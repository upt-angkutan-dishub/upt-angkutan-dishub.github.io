BANDROS Ride Guide

Silahkan datang langsung ke tempat naik & turun :

  • Alun Alun Kota Bandung / Seberang Pendopo Kota Bandung
    Google Map : https://goo.gl/maps/QeCCE7QKTFMDd7JH9
  • Jl. Diponegoro / Seberang Museum Geologi
    Google Map : https://maps.app.goo.gl/HwjMaJfbHdVh89kZA

Operasional Setiap Hari Jam 08:00 - 16:00 WIB
Keberangkatan terakhir ± 15:00 WIB

Waktu keliling ± 45 menit tergantung kondisi lalu lintas

Harga tiket Rp 20.000 /orang
Pembelian langsung di bus (Tidak menerima reservasi)

Apabila kondisi ramai akan digunakan sistem waiting list dengan cara mendaftarkan diri ke petugas dan nanti akan dipanggil.

Please come directly to the bus stop :

  • Alun Alun Kota Bandung / Across Pendopo Kota Bandung
    Google Map : https://goo.gl/maps/QeCCE7QKTFMDd7JH9
  • Jl. Diponegoro / Across Museum Geologi
    Google Map : https://maps.app.goo.gl/HwjMaJfbHdVh89kZA
Operational Every day at 08:00 - 16:00 WIB
Last departure ± 15:00 WIB

Travel time ± 45 minutes depending on traffic conditions
Ticket price IDR 20,000 / person

Ticket purchase directly on the bus (Does not accept reservations)

If conditions are crowded, a waiting list system will be used by registering with the officer and will be called later.
